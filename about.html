About ChemE Unlocked

Democratizing chemical engineering education, one concept at a time

Our Mission

ChemE Unlocked exists to democratize chemical engineering education by providing free, high-quality resources to students everywhere.

No paywalls. No prerequisites. No geographical barriers. Just knowledge, unlocked.

Whether you're studying in Africa, Asia, Europe, the Americas, or anywhere else in the world, you deserve access to quality educational resources that help you master chemical engineering concepts.

About the Founder

Built by a Chemical Engineering Student

ChemE Unlocked was created by a chemical engineering student at BIUST (Botswana International University of Science and Technology) who understands firsthand the challenges of mastering complex engineering concepts.

Why This Platform Exists

As a ChemE student, I've experienced the struggle of trying to understand difficult concepts with limited resources. I've seen how quality educational materials are often locked behind paywalls or unavailable in certain regions.

This platform was born from a simple belief: If I can learn it, I can explain it. And if I can explain it, we can all master it together.

ChemE Unlocked is my way of giving back to the global student community - sharing knowledge as I learn, breaking down complex topics into understandable pieces, and creating resources I wish I had when I started.

The Vision

To build a comprehensive, free, and accessible chemical engineering knowledge hub that serves students worldwide - from Gaborone to Mumbai, from Lagos to S√£o Paulo, from everywhere to anywhere.

This isn't just an African platform, or a platform for developing countries. This is a global platform for anyone who wants to learn chemical engineering, regardless of where they are or what resources they have access to.

"Education should be a bridge, not a barrier. Knowledge should be open, not locked. Learning should be accessible to all, not just a privileged few."
